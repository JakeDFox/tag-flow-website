Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

TagFlow is a Shopify app that automatically tags orders based on rules you define. This policy explains what data we access, why we access it, and how it is handled.

1. Who we are

TagFlow ("we", "us", "our") is a Shopify application operated as an independent software product. For questions about this policy, contact us at hello@tagflow.run.

2. What data we access

When you install TagFlow, we request access to the following Shopify data:

  • Orders — order details including line items, totals, shipping address, payment gateway, and applied tags. This is required to evaluate your tagging rules.
  • Customer data — customer email address and customer tags, used only when you create rules that condition on these fields.
  • Shop information — your store domain and access token, used to authenticate API requests on your behalf.

We do not access product inventory, financial reports, staff accounts, or any data outside the scopes listed above.

3. How we use your data

Data accessed from your Shopify store is used exclusively to:

  • Evaluate your tagging rules against incoming orders
  • Apply tags to orders via the Shopify Admin API
  • Store an audit log of rule runs for your reference
  • Display your rules and audit log within the TagFlow app interface

We do not sell, share, or use your store data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose outside of providing the TagFlow service.

4. Data storage

TagFlow stores the following data in our database:

  • Your shop domain and encrypted access token
  • Rules and conditions you create within the app
  • Audit log entries — order ID, order name, rule name, tag applied, source, status, and timestamp

We do not store full order objects, customer profiles, or payment information. Audit log entries contain only the minimum identifiers needed to display your log.

5. Data retention

Audit log entries are retained for 90 days by default. When you uninstall TagFlow, your shop data and all associated records are deleted within 48 hours.

6. Third-party services

TagFlow is built on Gadget.dev for hosting and infrastructure. Your data is processed and stored within Gadget's platform. Gadget's privacy policy is available at gadget.dev/privacy.

We use Resend to send transactional emails such as your welcome email on install. Resend's privacy policy is available at resend.com/privacy.

No other third-party services receive your store or customer data.

7. Security

Access tokens are encrypted at rest. All communication between TagFlow and Shopify occurs over HTTPS. We follow Shopify's security requirements for partner apps and handle webhook payloads using verified signatures.

8. Your rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@tagflow.run or by uninstalling the app from your Shopify admin. Uninstalling automatically triggers data deletion.

9. Shopify compliance

TagFlow is built in accordance with Shopify's API Terms of Service and Partner Program Agreement. We only request API scopes necessary for the app to function.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be communicated via email to the store owner. Continued use of TagFlow after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us at hello@tagflow.run. We aim to respond within 2 business days.
